About Our Agency

Founded in 2018 in Freetown, SL Football Agency has supported over 45 Sierra Leonean players in securing professional football contracts worldwide.

We work with trusted clubs across Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Americas, offering world-class player development programs and career management.

Our mission is simple: Every talented child in Sierra Leone deserves a chance to shine.

Our Mission

To identify, develop, and promote Sierra Leone’s brightest football talents.

Our Vision

To become West Africa’s leading football talent powerhouse by 2030.
